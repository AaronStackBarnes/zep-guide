Step by Step Guide for an Art Director Using Zeplin to Hand Off Designs for a Website from Photoshop

  1. Prepare Photoshop files:
    • Organize your layers and groups in a clean and logical manner.
    • Name layers and groups descriptively, so developers can easily understand the purpose of each element.
    • Use consistent naming conventions for similar elements.
    • Use vector shapes and smart objects for scalability.
    • Convert text layers to paragraph or character styles, and ensure all text uses these styles.
  2. Export designs from Photoshop to Zeplin:
    • Download and install the Zeplin Photoshop plugin.
    • Open your Photoshop document and select the artboards or layers you wish to export.
    • Navigate to File > Export > Zeplin or use the keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+Alt+Z).
    • Log in to your Zeplin account and choose the project you want to export the designs to, or create a new one.
    • Confirm the export settings and click "Export."
  3. Review and optimize the generated styles in Zeplin:
    • Open the exported designs in Zeplin.
    • Verify that the generated styles match your design intentions.
    • Make adjustments to styles and assets, as needed.
    • Ensure the generated styles are responsive by checking that they adapt well to different screen sizes, devices, and resolutions.
  4. Generate components:
    • Identify reusable elements in your design, such as buttons, icons, and headers.
    • Right-click on the element and choose "Create Component" from the context menu.
    • Name the component and add any necessary descriptions or notes.
    • Repeat for all reusable elements.
  5. Create and organize style guides:
    • In Zeplin, navigate to the "Styleguide" tab of your project.
    • Click "Add Styleguide" to create a new style guide.
    • Organize your components, colors, text styles, and assets in the style guide.
    • Add descriptions and notes for each element to provide context for the developers.
  6. Export style sheets and assets:
    • In the "Styleguide" tab, click on the "Export" button.
    • Choose the format you want to export the style sheets and assets in (e.g., CSS, SCSS, Less, or Stylus).
    • Configure any additional settings, such as including/excluding specific components or assets.
    • Click "Export" to download the generated style sheets and assets.
  7. Collaborate with the development team:
    • Invite developers to your Zeplin project by clicking the "Invite" button in the top-right corner.
    • Encourage open communication and collaboration through comments and annotations.
    • Regularly review and update designs, as needed, based on feedback from the development team.
